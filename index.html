About Me

System developer with special interest in front-end I started my career in tech after deciding to switch career from another sector, and I have never regretted it! Previously I worked in the social sector with research and evaluation. My interest in code started when I was customizing my MySpace page for my music. I found it very interesting, so I continued coding until I realized I wanted to do this full time!

My choice for learning more about technology lead me to studying computer science bachelor program at Uppsala university to get a deep and broad knowledge in the field.

As a creative person I always find ways to canalize my creativity such as music, arts and preferably combined with some tech skills as in sound technics. I have also programming as a hobby which has resulted in some apps on Google play. Beyond that I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. When possible I enjoy visiting restaurants and experiencing nice food in good company.
